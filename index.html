Welcome to ModFest 1.15!

ModFest 1.15 is a Fabric modding event running from March 28 to April 5.
There are three themes for mods to enter in: Exploration, Industry, and Magic.
You may participate solo, with your own team, or sign up for randomly-selected team.
All mods must be for the Fabric mod loader and target 1.15.2.
Mods must be newly created for ModFest and should loosely follow at least theme.
At the end of ModFest, mods will be ranked by the community and a modpack containing all jam mods will be released.
