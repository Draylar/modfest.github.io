ModFest Pack

The ModFest pack contains all ModFest entries so that players can experiment with them before voting.

Installation Instructions:

  1. Click "Add Instance" in MultiMC
  2. Go to "Import from zip" and paste in the download URL, or select the downloaded ZIP from your computer
  3. Launch once to initialize auto-updating, then launch again to run
  4. When the Voodoo dialogue appears, click "Install"

Additional Mods:

  • Roughly Enough Items
  • HWYLA
  • Tech Reborn
  • Lithium

This pack only works with MultiMC, due to many mods not being available on CurseForge.
